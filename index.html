Eat Right!: The Game!

  • High Concept: Eat enough broccoli to earn a cookie. Get score!
  • Player Experience Goals: Counting, and eating right.
  • Genre: Educationaru desu.
  • Game play: Arrow keys to move, m to pause music, n to resume music. You must collect broccoli for score. When you eat 10 broccoli, a red ! will appear, signaling that you have earned a cookie. You may eat a cookie for huge BONUS DUCKS! If you are greedy and eat too many cookies, you will lose a life. Play until lives are gone.
